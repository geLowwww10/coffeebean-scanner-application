Types of Coffee Beans

Arabica Coffee

Origin: Arabica coffee is believed to have originated in Ethiopia, where it was first cultivated more than a thousand years ago.

Global Coffee Production: 60%

Characteristics: Smooth, sweet, floral

Caffeine: 8-12 mg/g

Robusta Coffee

Origin: Robusta coffee is believed to have originated in sub-Saharan Africa, specifically in regions of Central and Western Africa.

Global Coffee Production: 40%

Characteristics: Bold, nutty, bitter

Caffeine: 18-20 mg/g

Excelsa Coffee

Origin: Excelsa coffee is a variety of Liberica coffee and is native to Southeast Asia, particularly the Philippines and Vietnam.

Global Coffee Production: <1%

Characteristics: Woody, earthy

Caffeine: 8-10 mg/g

Liberica Coffee

Origin: Liberica is native to West Africa but is now mostly cultivated in the Philippines, Malaysia, and Indonesia.

Global Coffee Production: <1%

Characteristics: Woody, fruity, sweet

Caffeine: 10-12 mg/g
